# VOA Constitution & Policies

> *Voice of Adolescents — Empowering Voices. Building Futures.*

---

## Preamble

We, the members of the **Voice of Adolescents (VOA)**, united by our shared commitment to youth empowerment, community development, and the promotion of adolescent rights, hereby establish this Constitution as the supreme governing document of our organization.

---

## Article I — Name & Identity

**1.1** The organization shall be known as **Voice of Adolescents**, abbreviated as **VOA**.

**1.2** The official motto is: *"Empowering Voices. Building Futures."*

**1.3** The official colors are Deep Blue (#1E3A8A), Orange (#F97316), and Green (#22C55E), representing Trust, Energy, and Growth respectively.

---

## Article II — Vision & Mission

### Vision
A world where every adolescent has a voice, opportunity, and the tools to shape their future.

### Mission
To empower adolescents through education, community engagement, leadership development, and advocacy — creating a generation of confident, responsible, and impactful citizens.

### Core Values
- **Integrity** — We act with honesty and transparency in all we do.
- **Inclusion** — Every voice matters, regardless of background.
- **Excellence** — We pursue the highest standards in our programs.
- **Collaboration** — We achieve more together than alone.
- **Impact** — We measure success by the change we create.

---

## Article III — Governance

### 3.1 Leadership Structure

The VOA shall be governed by the following leadership roles:

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|-----------------|
| **Chairman** | Overall leadership, final authority, strategic direction |
| **Secretary** | Records, minutes, correspondence, attendance |
| **Treasurer** | Financial management, budgets, reports |
| **PRO** | Public relations, announcements, communications |
| **Program Coordinator** | Plan and execute programs and events |
| **Membership Coordinator** | Member registration, approval, engagement |
| **Welfare Officer** | Member welfare, support, follow-up |

### 3.2 Vice Roles

Each leadership role may have a designated Vice who:
- Assists the primary officer in all duties
- Acts in the absence of the primary officer
- Has limited independent authority unless explicitly delegated

### 3.3 Elections

- Elections shall be held annually or as needed
- All active members in good standing are eligible to vote
- Candidates must have been active members for at least 6 months
- Results are determined by simple majority

---

## Article IV — Membership

### 4.1 Eligibility

Membership is open to all adolescents and young adults who:
- Are between the ages of 13 and 30
- Agree to uphold the VOA Constitution and values
- Complete the registration process

### 4.2 Member Rights

Every member has the right to:
- Participate in all VOA programs and activities
- Vote in elections (active members only)
- Access welfare support when needed
- Be treated with dignity and respect
- Raise concerns through proper channels
- Access the VOA Constitution at any time

### 4.3 Member Responsibilities

Every member is expected to:
- Attend programs and meetings regularly
- Contribute positively to the community
- Respect fellow members and leadership
- Uphold the VOA values at all times
- Pay any applicable dues on time
- Report misconduct or violations

### 4.4 Membership Status

- **Active**: Engaged member in good standing
- **Inactive**: Member with no activity for 14+ days
- Members may be reactivated by the Membership Coordinator

---

## Article V — Code of Conduct

### 5.1 Expected Behavior

All members must:
- Treat everyone with respect and dignity
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
- Represent VOA positively in public
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Communicate professionally

### 5.2 Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited:
- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind
- Misuse of VOA funds or resources
- Sharing confidential member information
- Spreading misinformation about VOA
- Conduct that brings VOA into disrepute

### 5.3 Disciplinary Process

1. **Warning** — First offense, verbal or written warning
2. **Suspension** — Temporary removal from activities
3. **Expulsion** — Permanent removal for serious violations

All disciplinary actions require review by the Chairman and at least two other officers.

---

## Article VI — Programs & Activities

### 6.1 Program Standards

All VOA programs must:
- Align with the VOA mission and values
- Have a designated Program Coordinator
- Maintain attendance records
- Produce a post-event report

### 6.2 Attendance

- Members are expected to attend at least 70% of programs
- Attendance is recorded by the Secretary
- Engagement scores are updated based on participation

---

## Article VII — Finance

### 7.1 Financial Management

- The Treasurer is responsible for all financial records
- All income and expenses must be documented
- Expenses above ₦10,000 require Chairman approval
- Financial reports are presented quarterly

### 7.2 Dues & Contributions

- Member dues are set by the leadership annually
- Dues support program delivery and operations
- Financial hardship cases may apply for exemption

---

## Article VIII — Welfare

### 8.1 Welfare Support

VOA is committed to the wellbeing of all members. The Welfare Officer shall:
- Receive and process welfare requests
- Maintain confidentiality of all welfare cases
- Provide follow-up support
- Escalate serious cases to leadership

### 8.2 Types of Support

- **Financial** — Emergency financial assistance
- **Personal** — Counseling referrals and emotional support
- **Other** — Any other welfare need as determined

---

## Article IX — Amendments

This Constitution may be amended by:
1. A written proposal submitted to the Secretary
2. Review by the full leadership team
3. Approval by a two-thirds majority of active members
4. A 30-day notice period before implementation

---

## Article X — Dissolution

In the event of dissolution:
- All assets shall be donated to a registered youth charity
- All member data shall be securely deleted
- A final report shall be published

---

*This Constitution was adopted by the founding members of VOA and shall remain in effect until amended or dissolved in accordance with the procedures herein.*

**Signed:** VOA Leadership Council
